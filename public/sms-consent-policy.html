SMS Consent Policy

Your Consent to Receive SMS Messages

By opting into our SMS service, you agree to receive automated text messages from Massage by Ivan for the following purposes:

  • Appointment reminders
  • Booking confirmations
  • Service updates
  • Important notifications about your appointments

How to Opt-In

When creating your account, you'll see this consent option:

SMS Communication Consent

Checking this box indicates your consent to receive our SMS messages.

How to Opt-Out

You can opt-out at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any SMS message
  • Contacting us at support@massagebyivan.com
  • Unchecking this option in your account settings
